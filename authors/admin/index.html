Jinbin Zhu （朱金彬）

Lecturer @ SDUST

Chip Verification Engineer @ BOSC (dual-employed)

Jinbin Zhu, male, obtained his Ph.D. degree in June 2023 from Beihang University, where he was actively involved in visiting research at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. In July 2023, he joined Shandong University of Science and Technology (SDUST), working as an associate professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering. After joining, he became a member of the Software Engineering Department, engaging in related teaching and research activities. He also served as the class advisor for the 2023 cohort and participated in assisting with the Computer System Architecture course. Additionally, he currently serves as a chip verification engineer (dual-employed) at the Beijing Open Source Chip Research Institute (BOSC), where he is responsible for chip verification tool development and related tasks.

His primary research interests include computer system architecture, cloud computing data center resource management, and artificial intelligence. To date, he has participated in several research projects, including 2 national key research and development programs, 1 National Natural Science Foundation project, and multiple projects funded by the National Defense Science and Technology Commission. Over the past five years, Jinbin Zhu has authored over 10 academic papers, including 2 first-author papers published in journals indexed by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, 1 paper in a second-tier journal, and 2 papers recommended by the China Computer Federation (CCF). He has also applied for more than 10 invention patents, with 7 already granted authorization.

朱金彬，男，博士研究生，2023年6月毕业于北京航空航天大学，期间长期参与中国科学院计算技术研究所的客座研究工作。他于2023年7月入职山东科技大学，在计算机科学与工程学院从事教师系列专业技术岗位（中级）的工作。入职后，他加入软件工程系并开展相关教学和科研工作，担任2023级班主任并参与计算机体系结构课程的助课工作。此外，他目前兼任北京开源芯片研究院的芯片验证工程师，负责芯片验证工具的开发以及相关任务。

他主要研究领域包括计算机系统结构、云计算数据中心资源管理以及人工智能等。截至目前，他已参与多项科研项目，包括2项国家重点研究发展计划项目、1项国家自然科学基金面上项目以及多项军科委项目等。近五年，朱金彬发表学术论文10余篇，其中以第一作者发表中科院一区论文2篇、二区论文1篇，以及CCF推荐中文A类和会议2篇。他已申请发明专利10余项，其中7项已获得授权。
